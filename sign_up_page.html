The International Society for

Universal Dialogue (ISUD)

International peace makes the world a better place. Countries around the world depend on one another for their progress and development, but that cannot be achieved without interaction and dialogue. For this purpose, International Society for Universal Dialogue (ISUD) was established in Warsaw, Poland in 1989 to achieve world order through dialogue and innovation. ISUD facilitates conferences to draw from and share the expertise of scholars from various disciplines to create universal dialogue to achieve the best possible outcomes for world security and order. The Society is devoted to promoting fundamental issues such as world peace, social justice, human rights, and diversity.   Sign up to learn more
