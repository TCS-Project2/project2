The International Society for Universal Dialogue (ISUD)

The International Society for Universal Dialogue was founded in Warsaw, Poland in 1989. ISUD is a philosophical society dedicated to promoting international and intercultural dialogue around the world. The main purpose of the Society is to pursue, by means of scholarly work the need for realization of a decent, peaceful, and just world order. To that end, ISUD produces biannual international conferences to facilitate an ethos of universal dialogue to attain the highest and richest human values worldwide.

ISUD is a member of the International Federation of Philosophical Societies (IFPS), which includes philosophers and scholars from various disciplines including history, archaeology, anthropology, etc. The Society is devoted in promoting fundamental issues such as world peace, social justice, human rights, and diversity.
